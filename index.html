I am currently a first year PhD student in Computer Science at Princeton University (where I also got my Master's degree), and I am fortunate to be advised by Matt Weinberg. My research interest include algorithmic game theory, approximation algorithms, graph algorithms, and theoretical computer science in general.

Prior to joining Princeton, I graduated with University Honors from University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign with a BS in Computer Science and a BSLAS in Mathematics.
